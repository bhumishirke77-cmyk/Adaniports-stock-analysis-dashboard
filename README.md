# Adaniports-stock-analysis-dashboard
An interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing the historical stock performance of Adani Ports and Special Economic Zone Ltd (ADANIPORTS) - one of India's largest port operators and a constituent of the NSE. This project is part of my data analytics portfolio.
# Project Overview
This dashboard visualized over 14 years of NSE stock data(2007-2021) for ADANIPORTS, covering price trends, trading volume, price range behaviour and market trunover. The goal was to extract the meaningful insights from raw market data using talbeau visualization capabilities.
# Charts
1.Avg close price over time (line chart)
-- tracks how the average closing price moved year over year from 2007 to 2021.
-- Helps idenitfy long term price growth and dips.
2.Volume by year (bar chart)
-- shows the total number of shares traded each year.
-- useful for spotting high activity years driven by market events.
3.Avg high VS Avg low over time (line chart)
-- compares the average daily high and low price per year.
-- wider gaps indicates higher volatitliy in that period.
4.Avg turnover over time (area chart)
-- visualizes the total market turnover trend across all years.
-- reflects overall investor interest and liquidity in the stock.
# Dataset
-- Source: National Stock Exchange of India 
-- File: ADANIPORTS.csv
-- Period:2007 to 2021
-- Date: Daily stock records
# Tools used
-- Tableau desktop for all visualizations and dashboard assembly
-- CSV file as the data source
# Key insights
-- closing price grew significantly after 2014 reflecting business expanison
-- stock market ptice was very low in early years (2007-2010) and kept growing
-- the gap between daily high and low prices increased over the years
-- turnover keep increasing year by year showing
